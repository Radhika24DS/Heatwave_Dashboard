title: Low Heatwave Advisory for the General Public
---
1. Stay hydrated by drinking water regularly, even if you don't feel thirsty.
2. Limit outdoor activities during the hottest part of the day (12 pm–4 pm).
3. Wear lightweight, light‑colored clothing and a wide‑brimmed hat.
4. Check local weather alerts and keep windows closed while using fans.
