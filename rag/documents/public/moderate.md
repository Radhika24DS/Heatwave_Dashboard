title: Moderate Heatwave Advisory for the General Public
---
1. Increase fluid intake; aim for at least 2‑3 L of water per day.
2. Schedule outdoor work or exercise for early morning or evening when temperatures are lower.
3. Wear breathable, moisture‑wicking clothing and a hat; use sunscreen with SPF 30+.
4. Monitor vulnerable individuals (elderly, children, chronic‑illness patients) for signs of heat‑related illness.
5. Keep indoor spaces ventilated; use fans or air‑conditioning if available.
6. Stay informed via local weather alerts and heed any heat‑stroke warnings.
